Hi! I am Sarah Lashinsky, an Atlanta native and a product designer at Strava.

I am a multidisciplinary designer, an ever-learning creative & your critical friend. I strive to "yes, and" my way through tough problems alongside engineers, researchers, and product management.

I coded this site 100% by hand as a personal project with the support of my friend, Brenton Strine.

  • An accessibility advocate
  • A tinkerer
  • A teacher

Mentorship
